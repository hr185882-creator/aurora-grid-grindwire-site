05 / Evidence protocol

A portfolio that tells you what it knows.

The operating standard is simple: distinguish records, inferences, uncertainty, and independent work before making a recommendation.

RL-01

Record lock

Professional claims stay bounded to supplied records. Missing evidence remains a gap, not an invitation to invent detail.

SB-02

Source hierarchy

Primary records lead; strong documentary corroboration follows; rumors and weak summaries are treated only as leads.

CB-03

Claim boundaries

Paid experience, demonstrated tools, and self-directed systems are labeled separately so reviewers can assess each fairly.

RC-04

Revision control

Contradictory evidence triggers an update. Confidence should move with records, not with attachment to an earlier answer.
